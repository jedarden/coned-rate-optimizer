Rate plans & assumptions

ConEd residential (SC1) plans priced here: Standard, Time-of-Use, Steady Use (demand-based · heat-pump homes), and Smart Energy (demand-based · any smart meter). The headline verdict compares the precisely-modeled Standard vs. Time-of-Use; the two demand-based plans are shown as ballpark estimates (they bill on peak kW, and their exact supply rates aren’t published). There is no separate residential EV rate — EV owners use Time-of-Use plus SmartCharge NY rebates.
